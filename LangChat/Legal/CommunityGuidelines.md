# Fluenca Community Guidelines

Last updated: January 2025

Welcome to Fluenca! Our community is built on the foundation of language learning and cultural exchange. These guidelines help ensure Fluenca remains a safe, respectful, and positive environment for everyone.

## Our Core Values

- **Respect**: Treat all members with dignity and kindness
- **Safety**: Keep our community safe for everyone
- **Authenticity**: Be genuine in your interactions
- **Learning**: Support each other's language learning journey

## Expected Behavior

### Be Respectful
- Use polite and appropriate language
- Respect cultural differences and perspectives
- Be patient with language learners of all levels
- Give constructive feedback when helping others

### Be Authentic
- Use real photos that accurately represent yourself
- Be honest about your language abilities
- Create only one account
- Don't impersonate others

### Be Supportive
- Help fellow learners improve their language skills
- Share knowledge and cultural insights
- Encourage others in their learning journey
- Celebrate progress, no matter how small

## Prohibited Content and Behavior

### Harassment and Bullying
- No harassment, bullying, or intimidation
- No threats of violence or harm
- No stalking or unwanted persistent contact
- No discriminatory language or hate speech

### Inappropriate Content
- No sexually explicit content or nudity
- No violent or graphic imagery
- No promotion of illegal activities
- No spam, scams, or misleading content

### Privacy Violations
- Don't share others' personal information
- Don't screenshot or share private conversations without consent
- Respect boundaries when others say no

### Misuse of the Platform
- Don't use Fluenca for commercial purposes or advertising
- Don't solicit money or financial information
- Don't attempt to circumvent our safety features
- Don't use automated systems or bots

## Reporting Violations

If you encounter content or behavior that violates these guidelines:

1. **Report the user or content** using the report feature
2. **Block the user** if you don't want further contact
3. **Contact support** for urgent safety concerns

We review all reports within 24 hours and take appropriate action, which may include:
- Warning the user
- Temporarily suspending the account
- Permanently banning the account
- Reporting to law enforcement if necessary

## Your Safety

### Protect Yourself
- Never share financial information
- Be cautious about sharing personal details
- Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, report it
- Consider keeping conversations within the app initially

### Meeting in Person
If you choose to meet someone from Fluenca in person:
- Meet in a public place
- Tell someone you trust about your plans
- Arrange your own transportation
- Trust your instincts

## Content Moderation

Fluenca uses a combination of automated systems and human review to:
- Filter potentially harmful content
- Review reported content and users
- Ensure compliance with these guidelines
- Maintain a positive learning environment

## Consequences

Violations of these guidelines may result in:
- Content removal
- Account warnings
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent ban

Severe violations may be reported to law enforcement.

## Updates to Guidelines

We may update these guidelines periodically. Continued use of Fluenca indicates acceptance of any updates.

## Questions?

If you have questions about these guidelines, please contact us at support@fluenca.app

Thank you for being part of our community and helping make Fluenca a welcoming place for language learners worldwide!
