# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 3, 2026**

PainKiller Labs ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Fluenca mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

**Account Information**
- Name (first and last)
- Email address (via Sign in with Apple)
- Username
- Profile photo(s)
- Date of birth
- Gender

**Profile Information**
- Bio/description
- Native language
- Languages you're learning
- Language proficiency levels
- Location (city)
- Language learning preferences

**User Content**
- Messages sent to other users
- Messages sent to AI Muses
- Feedback and support requests

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Device Information**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- App version

**Usage Information**
- Features used and interactions
- Time and duration of sessions
- Messages sent and received (count)
- Matches and connections made

**Location Information**
- General location (city level) based on IP address or device location services
- You can control location sharing in your device settings

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties

**Sign in with Apple**
- Apple ID (anonymized if you choose to hide your email)
- Email address (or relay email if hidden)
- Name (if provided)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

### 2.1 Provide and Improve the Service
- Create and manage your account
- Match you with other users based on language preferences
- Enable messaging and communication features
- Provide AI-powered features (translations, grammar checking, AI Muses)
- Process text-to-speech requests
- Respond to your requests and support inquiries
- Analyze usage to improve the App

### 2.2 Safety and Security
- Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms
- Verify user identity
- Protect the safety of our users and the integrity of the Service

### 2.3 Communication
- Send service-related notifications
- Notify you of matches and messages
- Provide updates about the App and your account
- Respond to your inquiries and feedback

### 2.4 Subscription Management
- Process and manage your subscription
- Track feature usage against subscription limits

## 3. How We Share Your Information

### 3.1 With Other Users
When you use the App, certain information is visible to other users:
- Profile information (name, photo, bio, languages)
- Location (city, if enabled)
- Online status
- Messages you send to them

We share information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating the App:

| Provider | Purpose | Data Shared |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| Supabase | Database and authentication | Account data, messages, profile info |

| OpenRouter/AI Providers | Translation and grammar | Message text for processing |
| Apple | Authentication, payments | Apple ID, subscription data |

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the services they provide to us.

### 3.3 For Legal Purposes
We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe in good faith that such action is necessary to:
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect our rights or property
- Prevent fraud or illegal activity
- Protect the safety of users or the public

### 3.4 Business Transfers
If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

### 3.5 With Your Consent
We may share your information for other purposes with your explicit consent.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Data Storage
Your data is stored on secure servers provided by:
- **Supabase:** Profile data, messages, and app data (hosted on AWS)
- **Supabase Storage:** Profile photos and media

### 4.2 Data Retention
We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. After account deletion:
- Profile information is deleted within 30 days
- Messages may be retained in anonymized form for safety and abuse prevention
- Some data may be retained as required by law

### 4.3 Security Measures
We implement appropriate security measures including:
- Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Encryption of data at rest
- Secure authentication via Sign in with Apple
- Regular security assessments
- Access controls for employee access to data

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Access and Update
You can access and update your profile information at any time through the App settings.

### 5.2 Delete Your Account
You can delete your account using the "Delete Account" feature in Settings > Data & Privacy. This will:
- Delete your profile and personal information
- Remove your photos from our storage
- Unlink you from all matches
- Delete your message history

### 5.3 Data Export
You can request a copy of your data by contacting us at support@painkillerlabs.com.

### 5.4 Location Settings
You can control location sharing:
- In your device's Settings > Privacy > Location Services
- In the App's profile settings (show/hide city)

### 5.5 Notification Preferences
You can manage push notifications through your device settings.

### 5.6 Communication Preferences
You can opt out of promotional communications by contacting us. Note that you will still receive service-related messages.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users who are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will delete it promptly.

If you believe we have information from a child under 18, please contact us at support@painkillerlabs.com.
